## APIs

### Finout
Finout is a FinOps platform that gives companies end-to-end visibility and control over cloud and SaaS spend. It unifies costs from multiple providers into a single, normalized view, then enriches and allocates that spend to teams, services, environments, and customers—even when native tagging is incomplete—so organizations can run accurate showback/chargeback and track unit economics like cost per feature or per customer. Finout offers budgeting, forecasting, and anomaly detection, and integrates with major clouds, Kubernetes, and data platforms to connect engineering activity to financial outcomes. The result is shared, actionable cost insights for finance, engineering, and product teams to reduce waste and make smarter, cost-aware decisions.
