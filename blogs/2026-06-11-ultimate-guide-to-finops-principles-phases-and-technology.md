Key Takeaways Definition: FinOps is a cultural practice and financial management discipline designed to maximize the business value of cloud investments. Lifecycle: The process follows a continuous "Crawl, Walk, Run" approach through three phases: Inform, Optimize, and Operate. Core Principles: Success relies on cross-functional collaboration, real-time visibility, and shared accountability for cloud spend.
