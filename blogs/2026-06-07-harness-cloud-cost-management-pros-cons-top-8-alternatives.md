This guide examines Harness Cloud Cost Management, a platform offering granular cost visibility, forecasting, and optimization across multi-cloud environments including AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. The article details key features such as anomaly detection and commitment management, outlines three pricing tiers with cloud cost tools limited to Enterprise, and presents eight notable alternatives ranging from dedicated FinOps platforms like Finout and CloudZero to broader solutions including Datadog and Flexera One.
