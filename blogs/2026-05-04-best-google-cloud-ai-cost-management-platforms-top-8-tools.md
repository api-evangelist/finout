What Are Google Cloud Cost Management Platforms? Google Cloud Cost Management Platforms are specialized tools to help organizations monitor, analyze, and control their spending on Google Cloud services. These platforms provide a centralized view of resource usage, expenses, and billing patterns, allowing IT, finance, and operations teams to better understand where money is being spent across projects and departments.
