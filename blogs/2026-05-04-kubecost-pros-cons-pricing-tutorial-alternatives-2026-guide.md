What Is IBM Kubecost? IBM Kubecost is a cost monitoring and optimization solution tailored for Kubernetes environments. It helps organizations track, allocate, and manage infrastructure expenses at a granular level, including per namespace, deployment, service, or label. By providing real-time visibility into cost trends and breaking down resource consumption, Kubecost allows teams to identify high-spend workloads and potential areas for savings within Kubernetes clusters.
