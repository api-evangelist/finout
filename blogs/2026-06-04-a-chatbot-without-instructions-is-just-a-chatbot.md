Chatbots require business-specific policies and guardrails to operate effectively within organizational contexts rather than functioning as generic assistants. The article argues that for AI to deliver real FinOps value, it must be trained on organizational cost structure, tagging conventions, budgets, and thresholds before it can provide actionable guidance rather than generic cloud cost advice.
