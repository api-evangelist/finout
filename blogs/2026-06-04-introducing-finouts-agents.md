Finout has introduced three AI-powered agents designed to automate FinOps workflows: the Detector identifies cost anomalies, the Investigator performs root-cause analysis, and the Orchestrator executes fixes. These specialized agents operate on Finout's unified data layer to handle repetitive tasks, allowing FinOps teams to focus on strategic decisions rather than manual investigation and remediation.
